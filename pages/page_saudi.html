The culture of Saudi Arabia maintains ancient traditions, mixed with influences from abroad and evolving with modern times.

Cultural clothing: Thawb

Thawb and the Bisht (which have variations according to the regions) are traditional clothing worn on important occasions. A thawb is a long, loose robe worn daily by Arab men, often white and lightweight. A bisht is a ceremonial cloak, richly embroidered and worn over the thawb for special occasions, symbolizing prestige and elegance.

Cultural food: Rice

Rice plays a central role in Filipino cuisine. Many types of rice are eaten throughout the Philippines, many being local to a specific area.
